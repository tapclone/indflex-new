About Us

In short “INDFLEX”, is Kerala’s first and only ISO 9001:2015, NSIC Certified MSME unit, manufacturing hydraulic & industrial hose assemblies. Established in 1996, over the years INDFLEX has built an everlasting relationship with its customers, extending them the highest quality product & services. The assemblies manufactured at INDUS are used in automotive, industrial, marine, construction, earthmoving & domestic applications. INDFLEX is equipped with Crimping Machines: Gates – GC32XD & POWER CRIMP 3000 and Uniflex with crimping range ¼” to 2 ½” ID withstanding a pressure upto 10,000 psi. Lot of weightage is given to the quality aspect since INDFLEX supplies to all major and minor industries across Kerala.

INDFLEX has been the backbone to the replacement market supplying hose assemblies for:

  • All Earth Moving Machineries: JCB, HITACHI, L&T KOMATSU, BEML, VOLVO, SCHWING STETTER, CATERPILLAR, HYUNDAI, DOOSAN, KOBELCO, KUBOTA, MAIT, GODREJ, SANI, CUMMINS, ROCK BREAKERS, TIPPERS.
  • Automobile, Air Conditioning Application, Fuel, Speed Governor Unit, Power Steering Unit.
  • Marine Trawlers, Sea Food Units, High Pressure Cleaners, Food Processing, Service Station
  • Refineries, Plywood Units, Tyre industries, Petrol Pumps, Steel Industries, Ship Building
  • Ready Mix Concrete Batching Plants, Piling Sites, Cement Industries, Generators etc.

INDUS TRADE LINKS since its’ inception, is the sole distributor and assembler for Gates products in Kerala. Gates Corporation USA, founded in 1911, is the world’s leading manufacturer of power transmission belts and a premier global manufacturer of power products (industrial & hydraulic hoses/end fittings and power transmission belts). A subsidiary of the parent company, Gates India Ltd; from Lalru, offers a complete portfolio of premium product and service solutions for both replacement and first-fit applications across targeted end markets, which encompass process and speciality, construction, agriculture, energy, transportation and automotive.

TRELLEBORG SPECIALIZED HOSES

We are Authorized Dealers and Assemblers of Trelleborg, France made specialized hoses. Though they have a wide range of hoses catering to various mediums and applications, we have started with their 4 category hoses – Fda Approved Food Grade Hoses, Chemical Transfer Hoses, Construction related hoses like Concrete Elephant and Cement Delivery Hoses and finally Oil and Petroleum Hoses. FLEXCO MECHANICAL FASTENING- A GIFT TO THE CONVEYING INDUSTRY

Flexco has developed real solutions to any conveyor belt challenges coming under the conveying industry. By constantly driving technology and design, Flexco strive to become the leader in belt conveyor solutions that maximise uptime, productivity and safety. To support the needs of Flexco customers, they partner with a network of the best distributors in every market they serve around the world. Through Flexco conveyor belt mechanical fastening, the production loss is minimized and fastening reduces downtime when compared to the endless conveyor belts and belt vulcanizing process which is time consuming. Indus is the authorised distributor and fasteners for Flexco Conveyor Belt products in Kerala. We send our technical teams to the customer sites for taking belt measurement and undergo the belt fastening process.
