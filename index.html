Our Services And Solutions

We supply a wide range of customized hydraulic and industrial hose assemblies suitable for different mediums of application, at specific temperature and pressure. Sample verification at customer sites can be offered to the clients based on request.

We have a wide range of SS corrugated bellow hoses which are used in extreme high temperature, which are tig welded and supplied after necessary testing. The medium of application is mostly gases, steam and acids

Our product line includes food grade, chemical, construction and oil & petroleum transfer hoses - FDA approved Food Grade Hoses for all kinds of food, Chemical hoses for acids and Construction Hoses for cement feeding and concrete loading/unloading purpose

We are active suppliers of all kinds of conveyor belts - rubber(plain & rough top), pu, pvc, cotton canvas including food grade type. Indus also undertakes fastening of all these kinds of conveyor belts using the Flexco belt joining tool. The industrial segments catered to includes Airport baggage, Packaging, Tyre manufacturers, Cattlefeed, Hospitals, Cement Plants, Mineral and Chemical Cos, Plywood, Crumb Rubber, Tea, Laundry, Rice Mill etc.
